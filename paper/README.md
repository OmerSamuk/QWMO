## Abstract

Quantum Wave-function Metaheuristic Optimizer (QWMO) is a population-based optimization algorithm inspired by probabilistic concepts from quantum mechanics. The algorithm combines adaptive orbital sampling, Pauli-inspired exclusion, and adaptive quantum escape operators to balance exploration and exploitation in multimodal optimization landscapes. QWMO was designed as a modular framework that enables operator-level analysis through ablation studies while maintaining competitive optimization performance across continuous benchmark functions.
